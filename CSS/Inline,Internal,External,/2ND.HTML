Introduction

Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world, known for its strategy, skill, and sportsmanship. Originating in England, it is now passionately played and followed in countries like India, Australia, England, South Africa, and many others.

üìú Basic Rules

  1. The game is played between two teams of 11 players each.
  2. Each team bats and bowls in innings.
  3. Runs are scored by hitting the ball and running between wickets.
  4. The fielding team tries to get the batsmen out in various ways.
  5. The team with the most runs at the end of the match wins.
