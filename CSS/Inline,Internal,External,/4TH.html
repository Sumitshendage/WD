Ordered List: Football Match Rules

  1. Each team has 11 players.
  2. Match duration is 90 minutes (two halves of 45 mins).
  3. Goals are scored by putting the ball into the opponent's net.
  4. No use of hands (except goalkeeper in the penalty box).
  5. The team with the most goals wins.

Description List: Football Terms

Goalkeeper
The only player allowed to use hands to prevent goals.
Offside
A player is offside if they are nearer to the opponent's goal line than both the ball and the second-last opponent when the ball is played to them.
Penalty Kick
A direct free kick taken from the penalty spot after a foul inside the penalty area.
Corner Kick
A free kick taken from the corner of the field when the ball is last touched by a defending player before crossing the goal line.
